HOW TO PLAY:

  • You are Pacanvasman, the yellow guy. Move him with the arrow keys and eat all the orange dots!
  • Watch out for the red ghost, though: if Pacanvasman gets too close, he dies and you lose!
  • To evade the ghost, go through the open edges to warp to the other side!
  • If you want to restart the game, just refresh the page.
